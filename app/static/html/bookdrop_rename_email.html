Dear reader,

As of today, due to trademark reasons, the Kindlebox service has been renamed to BookDrop. From now on, kindlebox.me is obsolete. If you need to stop syncing your Dropbox folder or update your active Kindle devices, please visit the new site, getbookdrop.com.

What does that mean for you? Not much. You can continue using BookDrop by adding books to Dropbox/Apps/kindle-box. Users who register after today will have the folder Dropbox/apps/book-drop.

Thanks for your continued support, and happy reading!
